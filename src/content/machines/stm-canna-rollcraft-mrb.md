## Overview

The STM Canna RollCraft MRB ranks last in this guide, but that's in the context of commercial pre-roll machines running 20,000+ joints per day. For its actual target operator — a dispensary making the jump from manual production — it's the most efficient $3,500 you can spend.

At 10,000 joints per day, it doesn't compete with the machines above it. What it does is eliminate hand-rolling labor for small and early-stage operations at a price point that doesn't require capital planning or financing.

## Who Makes It

STM Canna builds the RollCraft MRB on the same reliability foundations as their commercial line. Operators who start here and scale typically upgrade to the RocketBox 2.0 or Pro — they already know the manufacturer and the support process.

## Operator Impressions

Overwhelmingly positive within its target segment. The consistent feedback: it does exactly what it promises, and for the price, that's enough to justify the purchase for any operation still rolling by hand.

## Total Cost of Ownership

The best TCO score in this guide. Lowest upfront cost, simple maintenance, and STM parts availability. For a small operation, the ROI is almost immediate.
