## Overview

The Hefestus AuraX is the only machine in this guide that handles cones, straight rolls, blunts, and infused pre-rolls on a single platform. For dispensaries running a diverse product menu, this eliminates the capital cost of a second machine and the operational complexity of managing two production lines.

The tradeoff is specialization. No single format output on the AuraX matches the best dedicated machine for that format — the Blackbird produces better straight rolls, and the Holy Roller produces more precise cones. But for an operator who needs all four formats, the AuraX is the only viable single-machine solution.

## Who Makes It

Hefestus is a newer entrant in the commercial pre-roll space, founded around the insight that dispensary product menus were expanding faster than existing machines could accommodate. The AuraX is purpose-built for format flexibility.

## Operator Impressions

Operators with complex product menus report significant operational simplification. The most common feedback: the infused format capability alone justifies the purchase for dispensaries already producing or planning infused pre-rolls.

## Total Cost of Ownership

Custom pricing complicates direct comparison. The relevant framing is versus the cost of two machines — a dedicated straight-roll machine plus a cone/blunt machine. The AuraX typically comes in below that combined figure.
