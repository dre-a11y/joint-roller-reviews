## Overview

The PreRoll-Er 200 is built for operators who treat pre-roll production as a compliance function, not just a throughput exercise. Its ±0.01g weight accuracy is the tightest published spec in this category and is the primary reason operators in regulated markets choose it over higher-throughput alternatives.

The machine fills cones and straight rolls using a precision dosing system with real-time weight feedback. Every unit is verified, not sampled. For markets where regulators audit product weights, this matters.

## Who Makes It

PreRoll-Er is a Canadian manufacturer with a strong presence in the licensed Canadian c*nn*bis market, where dosing compliance requirements are among the most stringent globally. Their machines reflect those requirements.

## Operator Impressions

The dominant theme in operator feedback is peace of mind in compliance audits. Operators in US markets with loose weight tolerance requirements often find the compliance overhead unnecessary — the machine does more than they need for the price.

## Total Cost of Ownership

At $275K–$325K with lower throughput than the Blackbird, the TCO case is only compelling in high-compliance markets where the cost of a compliance failure exceeds the premium over alternatives. For standard US commercial operations, there are more cost-effective paths to acceptable accuracy.
