## Overview

The Futurola Knockbox 300 is the easiest machine in this guide to walk up to and run. Futurola built it for operators who are automating for the first time, and the design reflects that — straightforward loading, intuitive controls, and a modular format that doesn't require a production line mindset.

Throughput peaks at approximately 300 cones per 90-second cycle under optimal conditions. Effective hourly output depends heavily on operator pace and material prep, making it more of a productivity multiplier for skilled teams than a set-and-walk-away production machine.

## Who Makes It

Futurola is a Dutch company and one of the most recognized cone brands in the cannabis industry. The Knockbox line extends their brand into the automation space, and for operators already buying Futurola cones, the integration is seamless.

## Operator Impressions

Consistently praised for ease of use and low training requirements. The most common critique: operators who scale past 5,000 joints per day quickly find the Knockbox constraining and upgrade to a purpose-built commercial machine.

## Total Cost of Ownership

Custom pricing complicates direct comparison, but the Knockbox typically comes in below full commercial automation systems. For operators in the early stages of scaling, it's a reasonable bridge investment.
