## Content Collection Approach

**Phase 1 — Manufacturer specs** (1–2 days)
Compile official specs, pricing, and marketing materials from each manufacturer's site. Flag any claims that can't be independently verified.

**Phase 2 — Industry publications** (2–3 days)
MJBizDaily, mg Magazine, Cannabis & Tech Today. Look for third-party throughput claims, operator case studies, and market context.

**Phase 3 — Community forums** (1–2 days)
Future4200 (CannaBusiness section), THCFarmer, Reddit (r/weedbiz, r/cannabusiness). Primary goal: real operator pain points and reliability reports not in manufacturer materials.

**Phase 4 — Expert/operator outreach** (optional, if time allows)
Short interviews with 2–3 dispensary operators who have used these machines. Adds legitimacy and quotable content.

## Editorial Tone & Voice
- Written for the procurement decision-maker, not a consumer
- Data-forward: lead with specs and numbers, explain what they mean
- Honest about tradeoffs: no machine is best at everything
- No fluff language ("revolutionary", "game-changing") — let the specs speak
- Short paragraphs, headers, and tables throughout — these are busy operators, not readers

---

## Publication Details
- **Name:** Joint Roller Reviews
- **Author attribution:** Published under "Joint Roller Reviews" (no individual byline)
- **Update cadence:** Monthly — maintained as new machines launch, prices change, or rankings shift
- **Disclosure:** No commercial relationships with any manufacturer; independent editorial

## RollPros #1 Rationale (editorial position)
RollPros Blackbird earns the #1 overall ranking through its unique TruRoll™ radial compaction technology — the only commercial machine that actually rolls joints rather than filling pre-formed cones. This produces measurably better airflow consistency and burn quality, which directly impacts end-customer experience and brand reputation. Given that consistency/accuracy carries the highest weight (25%) in our scoring model, and that product quality is increasingly a competitive differentiator for dispensaries, RollPros' genuine technical advantage supports the top ranking. The writeup will honestly acknowledge its tradeoffs: lower throughput than GreenBroz or Hefestus, and no cone or blunt format support.
