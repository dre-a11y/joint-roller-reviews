# Joint Roller Reviews

An independent editorial review site for dispensary operators and cannabis brand owners evaluating commercial pre-roll automation equipment.

## What This Site Is

Joint Roller Reviews is a data-forward comparison resource covering the 8 leading automatic joint rolling machines on the market. The site serves procurement decision-makers — not consumers — with honest evaluations based on a weighted scoring rubric covering throughput, consistency/accuracy, format flexibility, total cost of ownership, ease of operation, uptime/maintenance, and support.

No commercial relationships with any manufacturer. Rankings are earned, not bought.
